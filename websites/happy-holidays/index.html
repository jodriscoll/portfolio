Need doesn't take

a holiday.

Neither should giving.

In 2013, Shawmut had the privilege of volunteering for and contributing to many inspiring organizations. If you’d like to help any of them, please click the links below.

We’re proud to partner with these worthy organizations.

Ace Mentor Program

This year, three separate teams partnered with students to educate them on the Architecture/Engineering/Construction industry.

All Souls Friday Soup Kitchen

Shawmut helps serve over 2,500 meals annually to the hungry of New York City.

Alzheimer’s Association

Over 20 Shawmut employees, family members, and friends joined the Memory Builders team and participated in the Walk to End Alzheimer’s.

Amos House

Our Rhode Island office organizes monthly group volunteering trips to help feed those in need.

Big Sister Association of Greater Boston

Every week, a group of women from Shawmut volunteer time as Big Sisters.

The Children’s Trust

Shawmut has donated over 1,300 toys. We also assisted in transforming the Valuing our Children Center in Athol, MA—doubling the square footage and donating materials and labor.

Community Servings

Over 60 volunteers contributed a total of more than 300 hours. We also donated toward meals for the critically ill and their families.

Cradles to Crayons

Every month, Shawmut volunteers help assemble backpacks for homeless and low-income children. We also helped establish a more efficient way to take inventory and track donations.

Future Chefs

Shawmut donated labor and construction management services to build Future Chefs’ new world-class teaching kitchen space.

Make-A-Wish Foundation

Shawmut devoted over 300 man-hours in 2 days to build a fully-furnished tree house for a 7-year-old girl.

New Haven Home Recovery

Over the past year, our Connecticut office volunteers have taken part in backpack drives, adopting families over the holidays, and building a shed for the organization.

Pan-Mass Challenge

This year, 14 riders and 10 water stop volunteers all joined forces to raise funds and awareness for the fight against cancer.

Pieces of home

In addition to relaunching a website, Shawmut volunteers helped organize a donation drive for kids in emergency placement homes.

Rebuilding Together NYC

Shawmut volunteers spent 290 hours helping to rebuild NYC neighborhoods affected by Hurricane Sandy.

Room to Dream Foundation

This year, 16 Shawmut employees volunteered for a Room to Dream renovation. We were also a lead sponsor at their annual golf tournament: 20 employees played and 7 volunteered.

Rosie’s Place

Over 80 employees volunteered 240 hours to serve lunch to the women of Rosie’s Place.

Summer Search

Shawmut brought approximately 20 teens on a job site tour and organized a career-networking event for 50 more.

The Greater Boston Food Bank

This year, Shawmut was able to help The Greater Boston Food Bank provide 390 Thanksgiving meals to families in need.

The One Fund

Shawmut employees made contributions that are still being used to aid victims and families affected by the tragic events at the Boston Marathon.

The Posse Foundation

Our LA office recently formed a relationship with The Posse Foundation and is excited to work with them on a monthly basis.

YouthBuild Boston

We worked with 60 young people across various levels of YouthBuild Boston’s vocational training program. That’s 60 well-trained, highly motivated future A/E/C professionals.
